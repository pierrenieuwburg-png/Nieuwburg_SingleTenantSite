Our Story

From a cleaning company to a platform transforming South Africa's service industry.

Nieuwburg began more than 25 years ago as a small cleaning team with a simple promise:

deliver reliable service, respect people's spaces, and bring order where there is chaos. That promise built trust. Trust built reputation. And reputation built a company recognised across Cape Town for consistency, care, and professionalism.

But as the world changed, something became clear. Service businesses needed more than good cleaning teams. They needed better tools, better workflows, and better ways to manage their entire operation.

So Nieuwburg evolved.

Today, Nieuwburg Blitz is the next chapter of that legacy. A full Business in a Box platform built for South Africa's service entrepreneurs. It is everything we wished we had when we were running cleaning teams, scheduling staff, managing bookings, sending invoices, and dealing with the daily pressure of service work.

Our Mission

To give service businesses a better way to work. Running a service business should not feel scattered. Messages in one app, invoices in another, staff on WhatsApp, bookings everywhere else. It drains time, energy, and opportunity.

Nieuwburg Blitz brings it all together. One powerful, all-in-one system that handles every step of your workflow: Lead → Quote → Payment → Booking → Staff → Completion → Invoice.

For SMEs this means clarity, structure, and freedom. For clients it means trust, transparency, and seamless communication. Our mission is to support the people who keep South Africa's homes and businesses running: plumbers, electricians, cleaners, handymen, gardeners, appliance technicians, and every service professional in between.

What We Are Building

South Africa's first integrated service-business ecosystem. Nieuwburg Blitz is more than software. It is a connected two-sided platform designed to help businesses operate smarter and help clients find trusted professionals.

  • A complete operational system for quotes, invoices, bookings, CRM, calendars, and staff management
  • Automated workflows that save time and reduce errors
  • Professional digital documents and a branded client experience
  • A built-in marketplace designed to drive new leads

For homeowners and clients it offers a trusted place to find verified providers, clear pricing, transparent histories, secure payments, and fast online bookings.

Where We Are Going

A future where service businesses can thrive, not just survive. With real-time scheduling, intelligent quoting, mobile staff tools, client dashboards, and a verified marketplace, Nieuwburg Blitz is becoming the infrastructure that powers South Africa's service economy.

Our vision is simple: to make running a service business organised, efficient, and enjoyable. Just like good service should be.
