Welcome to the Blitz Dock

Your personal command center for managing all your Nieuwburg services. The help you need, without the hassle, is now even easier to manage.

Complete Control at Your Fingertips

The Blitz Dock is designed to give you a seamless overview of your services. Once you're logged in, you'll be able to:

  • View Your Schedule: See all upcoming and past cleaning appointments in a simple, clear format.
  • Manage Bookings: Need to reschedule a service? You can request changes directly from your dashboard.
  • Access Invoices & Quotes: Download PDFs of all your quotes and invoices for your records, anytime.
  • Update Your Details: Easily update your contact information and service address.

Our goal is to provide a fully transparent and easy-to-use platform that saves you time and keeps you informed.
